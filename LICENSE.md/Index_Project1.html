What is SMOG?

"Smog" is a mixture of both smoke and fog. Smog is usually produced by cars, energy usage, ovens, fireplaces and so on. A youtuber was so eager to talk about it that he made a series (Now deleted) and now a page. That person was me, CargoFern. I started testing out animation because I really wanted to, but still. SMOG is dangerous people. Deal with it.
